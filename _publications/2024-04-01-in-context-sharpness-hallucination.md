**Authors:** Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He

This paper presents a novel approach to **hallucination mitigation** in large language models. By analyzing inner representations, we discover that **in-context sharpness** can serve as an early alert signal for potential hallucinations.

Key contributions:

* A new inner representation perspective for understanding hallucinations in LLMs
* In-context sharpness as an effective alert mechanism
* Empirical validation across multiple LLM architectures and benchmarks
