**Authors:** Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He

This paper presents a novel method for **composing parameter-efficient modules** using arithmetic operations, enabling flexible and efficient model adaptation without retraining.

Key contributions:

* A new approach to combining LoRA and other parameter-efficient fine-tuning modules
* Arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, scaling) for module composition
* Demonstrated effectiveness across multiple NLP tasks and model architectures
