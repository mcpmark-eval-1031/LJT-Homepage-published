We discover a **universal truthfulness hyperplane** inside large language models that separates truthful from untruthful representations. This geometric insight enables new approaches to controlling LLM truthfulness during inference.

Key contributions:

* Discovery of a universal truthfulness hyperplane in LLM internal representations
* A novel inference-time intervention method based on this geometric property
* Extensive experiments demonstrating the effectiveness of the approach

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
