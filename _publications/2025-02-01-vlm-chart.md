We investigate the **perception bottleneck** in Vision-Language Models (VLMs) for chart understanding. Our findings reveal that current VLMs struggle primarily with visual perception of chart elements rather than logical reasoning about them.

Key findings:

* VLMs exhibit significant perception errors when parsing chart images
* The bottleneck is primarily perceptual rather than reasoning-based
* Improvements in visual perception directly translate to better chart understanding performance

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He
