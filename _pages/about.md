## Personal Information

I am **Junteng Liu**, a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.cse.ust.hk/), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://junxianhe.github.io/). I completed my B.Eng. at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) (SJTU) in June 2024.
